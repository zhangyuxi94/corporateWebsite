Achievements

The new products of HELI independent research ＆ development have received a great deal of honors and awards.

>   National Special Equipments Manufacture License
The main products of HELI have passed National Special Equipments Manufacture License Certification of General Administration of Quality Supervision,Inspection and Quarantine of the People's Republic of China.

>   ISO9001: 2000 Certification
The main products’ design, manufacturing and service of HELI meet the ISO9001: 2000 Quality Management System standard, and have passed the certification of China Quality Certification Center.

>   ISO 14001:2004 Environmental Management System Certification
The main products’ design, manufacturing and service of HELI meet the ISO 14001:2004 Environmental Management System standard, and have passed the certification of China Quality Certification Center.

>   EC Type-examination Certificate
The main products of HELI have passed EC Type-examination, compliance with the EU Safety, Health, Environment, Consumer Protection and a series of provisions, that can be marketed in the European Union.

>   EPA Certification
The main products of HELI have passed EPA Certification of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency.
