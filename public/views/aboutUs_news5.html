HELI Loaders are going global

Date: 2014-08-29

As the largest R&D and manufacturing enterprise of Industrial Handling Trucks in China, HELI has been involved in the construction machinery industry since 2004. After a decade of development, loaders under the HELI brand are now being successfully promoted to markets all over the world.

HELI loaders have successfully been sold in many countries and regions in Middle East, Africa and South America.

As a major part of the company’s strategic development plan, HELI constantly pays close attention to developments in the construction machinery industry. Taking advantages of its own R&D and manufacturing capabilities, HELI has invested a total RMB0.22 billion in the loader industry, on a 300 acre site, which gave the company the ability to manufacture 20,000 wheel loaders annually.

As a result of the efforts of its professional R&D team, all HELI loaders are of original designs, and feature an ergonomic structure and industry-leading aesthetics.

The chosen ZF power shift transmission represents world-class technology, and the proprietary power shift transmission gears have outstanding performance, providing all of today’s technologies and making it possible for people to operate loaders in an economical, effective and safe way. Depending on the service conditions and the highest speed, there are four forward gears and four backward gears for you to choose from, so as to minimise shifting shock and maximise operator comfort.

Currently, HELI has 2 ton, 3 ton, 5 ton and 6 ton loader models and a total of 14 models to meet market demand.

While focusing on improving product quality, HELI has created a specialised sales team to respond to marketing feedback and further improve the timeliness of services. On top of that, agent conferences are held on a regular basis to share experiences and collect the latest marketing information.
