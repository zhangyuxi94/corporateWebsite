HELI's new products shown at CeMAT 2014

Date: 2014-07-03

The CeMAT event was held at Hannover Exhibition Center from the 19th to the 23rd of May, 2014. HELI attended with its full series of new products.

The new products HELI brought to CeMAT this time were well-prepared and fully demonstrated the highest design level of this national brand, which is leading the development trend for domestic industrial vehicles. The display included two iconic exhibition products: H-series 2-3.5 T internal combustion counterbalance forklift truck and the G-series 1-2.5 T four-wheel electric balance forklift.

The suspended bridge structure is adopted in the new H-series forklifts, dramatically decreasing the vibration noise of vehicles. The space layout of the vehicle is ideal; the ergonomic structure is excellent, handling comfort is good, highly reliable and safe. The performance of the vehicle increases substantially, and its major parameters have reached the level of first class international brands.

New H-series 2-3.5 tonne internal combustion counterbalance forklift truck.

G-series 1-2.5 T four-wheel electric balance forklift is the featured product that was developed independently by HELI. The performances of this series of models increases substantially. The vehicle has excellent stability and high flexibility, reasonable space layout and enhanced operational comfort. The main performance parameters are market-leading; the excellent quality and competitive price were popular and attracted international interest.
