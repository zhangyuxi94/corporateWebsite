2014 HELI middle east agent conference

Date: 2014-03-11

To strengthen communication and exchanges minds between HELI agents in the Middle East, carry out HELI international strategy, expand international market, make sales market in the Middle East become bigger and stronger, the agents from Saudi Arabia, Qatar, the U.A.E, Iran, Bahrain, Oman, Cyprus, Jordan came to Hefei participate in the 2014 HELI Middle East Agent Conference on 20th and 21th February. Leaders of Anhui HELI Co., Ltd Zhang Dejin, Yang Anguo, Xue Bai, Ma Qingfeng, Deng Li, Zhang Yingquan warmly received the guests, visited divisions together with them and joined the discussions.

Our agents visited manufacturing base of forklifts and key parts like Transmission division, Electric forklifts division, Internal combustion forklifts division successively accompanied by General Manager Yang Anguo during the visit, and also the trial production workshop, technology research and development center of the related research institute and products exhibition hall, experienced the strength of technology research and development closely.

President Zhang Dejin made the welcoming speech on behalf of HELI in the welcome meeting on the morning of Feb. 20th. General Manager Yang Anguo made the brief introduction of HELI to the guests. In the afternoon, Chief Engineer Ma Qingfeng focused on the future development trend of world industrial vehicle, introduced the new products with superior performance, flexible operation of HELI in 2014. Sales mangers from HELI Ningbo Liftstar Company and Anhui HELI Industrial Vehicle Imp.&Exp. Co., Ltd made the detailed introductions of Electric Warehouse Trucks, Electric Forklifts, Internal Combustion Forklifts, wheel loaders ,Tractors, heavy-duty products to them.

Accompanied by Marketing Director Deng Li, they visited Hefei Casting and Forging Factory on the Feb. 21th, experienced the casting manufacturing process closely, learned the technologies of V-casting, static pressure casting, resin sand casting, and lost-foaming casting, also watched simulated operation of wheel loaders and heavy equipment in the meantime. During the visit, the factory headers mainly introduced the key process equipment, technical innovation and key products, and exchanged views in deeply about the design idea, technical innovation and manufacturing technology with the guests. In the summing-up meeting afternoon, Marketing Director Deng Li made a passionate speech, then HELI leaders Zhang Dejin, Yang Anguo, Deng Li awarded to best sales agents, best service agents and best exploit agents of Middle East in 2013 and took pictures together. General Manager of Anhui HELI Industrial Vehicle Imp. & Exp. Co., Ltd Chen Jie gave introduction and report of the development of overseas market in the recent years and HELI international strategy in future. Five agents from Saudi Arabia, Qatar, the U.A.E, Iran and Oman exchanged experiences respectively, and made a warmly discussion with HELI’s sales staff and technical personnel.

Through this visit, the agents deeply understand the first-class level in product design; manufacturing process, production equipment and staff quality of HELI, greatly enhance confidence of Middle East agents to promote HELI’s products. International business teams of HELI will further expand into Middle East market, promote more and better products, provide more better service, cooperate friendly with agents to achieve "win- win", strive for " China HELI, lifting the future", compose the new chapter and create new brilliance for " Top 5 Worldwide, Centurial HELI, become a respected international first-class enterprise".
